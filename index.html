My name is Juliana Mota, I'm a Multimedia Designer student at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology (KEA) and I also have a master degree in Architecture from Lisbon University Institute.

In 2017, I decided to go back to school and learn more about design and coding. I keep on working with Adobe Indesign, Photoshop, Illustrator and Premiere. I am also learning building responsible website using tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON. With this studies, I'm also getting the chance to make myself familiar with designing documentation, preparing communication plans and learning about business.
